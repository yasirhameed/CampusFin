F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Teaching people about software product design and development is important to us. This includes answering all sorts of questions. So, here's what most people ask us starting from when should they choose us, type of works we do or how the process goes into a product.

  • What are your ways of working?

    We have always kept in mind the fact that we live in a world of technology, which is why we have incorporated it into our work. Agile is a flashy phrase; however, some of our teams will be similar to Scrum teams with conventional ceremonies. We are fully committed to Lean and Lean Startup principles such as people over process and validated learning through experimentation. Frequently, at clients’ premises or remotely, we employ scaled agile approaches like SAFe.

  • Are there any developers for the language or platform?

    We bring on board developers who can program in different languages and for various platforms after hiring them as generalists. The common technologies employed these days comprise .NET, Java, C++, React, AWS, Azure, Android and Python. We also have time to handle IOT and different embedded systems too. One can consult our Tech Details page containing all the programming languages and technologies used in recent product development initiatives.

  • Do you possess full-stack developers?

    Sure! Full-stack engineers are our kind. Rather than breaking down our developers into “front-end only” or “back-end only” roles, we do it differently.

  • Does the software have to be tested?

    Of course, it does. This is a must for the success of any software application before its launch with Quality Assurance and Testing.

  • Why should I get CampusFin QA & testing services?

    Our professional merciless bug slayers assure you high quality. We begin from the scratch as product guides that not only test but also carry out business analysis, safety and optimization. Tell us what to test!

  • Could my software be without bugs?

    Chances are that your software does not have bugs is very minimal. Why? Its development involves people who can make mistakes. There exist numerous functioning parts in even the simplest part of a software application which in turn contains multiple lines of intricate code. The creation of bugs may be due to poor integration among individual units at times leading to this situation. Therefore, QA & testing must happen.

  • How would QA & testing benefit my business?

    QA & testing is very essential in any project because it helps to keep your client's trust and also assists you to deliver efficient and high-quality products. Hire our experienced and talented QA engineers who are skilled merciless bug hunters ensuring you quality. They are the guardians of your product who stay active from the very beginning till the end of each process, participating not just in testing, but also business analysis, security as well as optimization.

  • Tell me about your software development services?

    We offer the next set of services separately or as one project development lifecycle such as: Branding and UI/UX Design, Web Development, Mobile App Development, QA & Testing Services, DevOps, Machine Learning, AWS Web Services etc.

  • Can I get other services apart from Software Development?

    Yes, we're also going to help you with your strategy for product design and digital transformation.
